Spell is a native iOS spelling game, fully playable offline. All content and game logic ship in the app bundle.

How to test:
1. Tap the glowing orb ("tap to hear a word") to hear a word spoken aloud.
2. Spell it on the on-screen keyboard and tap "Check spelling."
3. Correct answers grow your chain; try the Definition/Sentence hints.
4. Offline: enable Airplane Mode and keep playing — recently-heard words still play from the on-device cache.

No account, login, or personal information is required. No demo account needed.

Privacy: no personal data is collected. Progress and any custom word lists are stored only on the device. See https://spellgame.net/privacy.
