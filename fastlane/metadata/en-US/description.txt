Spell is a spelling game you play with your ears. Hear a word, then spell it — by typing or writing the letters by hand. Keep your chain going and climb from easy words to expert ones.

WHY SPELL
• Learn by listening. Every word is spoken clearly, so you practice going from sound to spelling — the skill real spelling tests measure.
• Type or write. Answer on the built-in keyboard, or draw the letters with your finger.
• Climb the chain. Words get harder as your streak grows; miss one and it returns later on a spaced-repetition schedule.
• Understand words. After each answer you see the word's meaning and an example sentence.

MADE TO FEEL GOOD
• Kid Mode and a Big Text option for younger or low-vision players.
• Head-to-head mode: pass one device and see who builds the longest chain.
• Works offline after the first load. No ads. No account required.

Spell turns "I want to spell more words better" into daily practice you'll actually keep up.
