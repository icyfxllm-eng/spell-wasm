Spell — Privacy Policy

Effective 9 July 2026

Spell is a spelling game published by the maker of spellgame.net. We built Spell to be private by design. This policy explains, in plain language, exactly what happens to information when you use the app or the website.

The short version: Spell has no accounts and no sign-in. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information. Your progress stays on your device. We show no ads and use no third-party analytics or tracking.

Information we do not collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account of any kind.
  • No location, contacts, photos, or advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking software.

Information stored on your device only

Your gameplay — streaks, statistics, settings, and any custom word lists you create — is saved in your device's local storage. It stays on your device, is never sent to us, and is removed if you delete the app or clear its data.

What the app sends to our server

To play a word, the app asks our server (spellgame.net) for that word's pronunciation audio and dictionary definition. Only the dictionary word is sent — never anything about you. Our server generates and caches audio for common words (using Google Cloud Text-to-Speech) so the app loads quickly; this cache is by word, not by user. All traffic uses HTTPS encryption. We keep standard, short-lived server logs for security and reliability; these are not used to identify or profile you.

Microphone and camera

The optional "speak the word" feature uses your device's own built-in speech recognition to hear your answer. Spell does not record, store, or transmit your voice to our servers. The optional handwriting feature recognizes letters you draw on your own screen; the drawing is processed on your device and is not uploaded.

Notifications

If you turn on the daily practice reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device. We do not run a push-notification server and cannot send you messages.

Children's privacy

Spell is suitable for all ages and is designed so that no personal information is collected from anyone, including children. Because we collect no personal data, there is nothing about a child (or anyone) for us to store, sell, or share.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how the app handles information, we will update this page and revise the date above. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.
